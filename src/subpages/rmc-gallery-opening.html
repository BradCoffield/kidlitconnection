Erika Wilson's RMC Gallery Opening

Please enjoy viewing Erika's “Illustrating Montana's Memory Day” Exhibit's Opening Reception at Rocky's Ryniker-Morrison Gallery in Tech Hall, held on Friday, January 14, 2022. The Gallery's Director is Todd Forsgren. Erika will be teaching a Printmaking course at the College in the Spring of 2023, with in-studio classes held in her Windy Mill Press printing shop across the street from Tech Hall.
